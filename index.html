3. Emergency Action Plan (EAP)

"NO DUFF" PROCEDURE

The phrase "NO DUFF" indicates a real emergency exists outside the training scenario. All training will immediately stop. All personnel shall report to staging area unless aiding the injured.

Incident Command: In a "No Duff" emergency, the Lead Instructor assumes IC. The Safety Officer notifies emergency services via radio/cell.

Weather: Training suspended during severe weather, electrical storms, or high wind events.

Add-On: Live Fire

1. SITE-SPECIFIC HAZARDS & "NO-GO" TRIGGERS

Structural Integrity

MUST be confirmed sound by inspector/engineer. Monitor for indicators of impending collapse.

Hazmat & Heavy Objects

Asbestos/combustibles removed. Heavy objects on upper floors/roofs removed to prevent collapse.

Utility Disconnection

Confirm all gas and hydro utilities are physically disconnected from the structure.

Approved Fuel Assessment

Wood/paper only. NO flammable liquids, foam padding, or foam insulation permitted.

Add-On: Water Rescue

Swift / Surface / Ice

1. SITE-SPECIFIC HAZARDS & "NO-GO" TRIGGERS

Water Current Limits

Verified upon arrival and every 30 mins. Max 1 knot (3 km/h). Immediate No-Go if forcing personnel under ice.

Ice Integrity & Open Leads

Thickness tested prior to entry. Pick-offs forbidden at closed ends of open leads without downstream safety.

Environmental & Weather

Continual monitoring for frost nip, frostbite, or hypothermia based on wind chill zones.

Physical Traps & Debris

Assess for floating debris (ice pans, trees) and steep road-to-ice transitions (slips/falls).

Add-On: Rope Rescue

1. SITE-SPECIFIC HAZARDS & "NO-GO" TRIGGERS

Weather Conditions

Assess for inclement weather, electrical storms, or high winds.

No-Go: Winds exceeding 80 km/hr.

Utility Hazards

Identify and isolate overhead Hydro lines or Natural Gas utilities in the operational drop zone.

Height & Edge Hazards

Identify fall risks (< 3m and > 3m). Assess for sharp edges, friction points, access challenges, and erosion.

Line of Sight & Comms

Identify areas where participants are out of sight or where communication challenges exist.

Add-On: Confined Space

1. SITE-SPECIFIC HAZARDS & "NO-GO" TRIGGERS

Atmospheric Hazards

Prohibited until tested for flammability, toxicity, asphyxiation, and oxygen deficiency.

Stored Energy (LOTO)

Identify all mechanical, electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic sources. LOTO required before entry.

Physical & Human

Assess entrapment, engulfment, sharp edges, and human factors (panic, exhaustion).
